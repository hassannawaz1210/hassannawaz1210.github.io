About TuneTube:

TuneTube is your melody sanctuary on Android, offering a symphony of features to enhance your listening experience. Craft custom playlists, seamlessly explore new tunes via YouTube integration, and let the music play on with background functionality. Built with Flutter for a sleek design and lightning-fast performance, TuneTube is your lightweight companion for musical bliss. Dive into a world of simplicity and creativity where every beat resonates with your soul.
